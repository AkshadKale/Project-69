School Achievments

Interschool Cricket Championship

My cricket team won the inter-house cricket championship.Every year our school conduct sports championship between four houses.The houses are:-Exploring Minds,Challenginig Minds,Royal Minds,Global Minds.I was in Royal Minds.And our house won the cricket championship.We played our final against Challenginig Minds,and they were very good players.But yet we won.







Debate Competition

Every year debate competition is held by our school between houses.The debate competition is only for students older than 10 years.Last year I participate in it,and luckily we won,our house won.The topics are different for every year.Last year the topic was "Different types of pollution by factories their causes,destruction,need and solution"







Tree Plantation

Another school activity which we did last year was tree plantation.We were really excited for that.First school buses took us to the spot where we have to plant trees.We had to plant bamboo trees.Our principal told us importance of planting trees,they told us by planting trees we can stop soil erosion.Each house were provided with a areato plant trees and two teachers.Our house planted nearly 1,200 bamboo plants.And all four houses together plant nearly 5,000 bamboo trees.
